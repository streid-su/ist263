My Story

Life can be very stressful sometimes. As a person who overthinks alot so many of my struggles are caused by the pressure I put upon myself. It can be very hard to get out of a negative thought cycle when anxiety is holding you back.

I spent many years of my life holding myself back from doing what I truly wanted to do in life because I was letting life happen to me instead of for me.

Eventually I decided it was time to make some changes and stop holding myself back. Through practicing Mindfulness in my daily life, I found ways to prioritize my mental well-being, decrease anxiety and regulate my emotions.

Mindfulness Is...

  • Observing your life as it is happening
  • Accepting your current situation without judgement
  • Taking action based on what your feel in your heart rather than old habits or short term cenvenience
